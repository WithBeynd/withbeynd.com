Most people's finances look fine on the surface. Money comes in. Money goes out. But underneath, there are patterns — debts quietly costing more than you realise, goals drifting off track, subscriptions charging for things you forgot you had. Beynd breaks it open. It shows you what is really happening, with specific numbers and a clear plan you can actually follow.

What Beynd does

Built to guide, not just track

Beynd does not move your money. It coaches, analyses, and guides — before every moment that matters.

Subscription visibility
Seven days before any subscription renews, Beynd brings it to your attention with the annual cost and a clear choice. Cancel before it charges. Keep it if you use it. Your money, your decision — made at the right time.
Plan before the moment
Beynd builds a monthly plan from your income, debts, goals, and spending — then adapts it to who you are. Irregular earner. Supporting a family. Focused on clearing debt. The plan changes because your life is not generic.
Coaching that adapts
When you follow a suggestion, Beynd acknowledges it and moves forward. When you do not, it adjusts rather than repeating itself. The coaching learns from how you actually live, not how you planned to.
Overdue alerts
Beynd flags payments and goals that have slipped past their date, naming them specifically and telling you exactly what to do. Not a generic warning. A specific, actionable nudge at the right moment.
Debt intelligence
Beynd calculates exactly what your highest-interest debt costs you every month and every year, then tells you the specific overpayment that would cut years off it. Real numbers. Real impact.
Your data. Your device.
Everything you enter stays on your phone. No server. No cloud account. No data sold. Beynd is a coach, not a data business. Bank integration is coming — until then, your financial life stays private.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Beynd Ltd ("Beynd", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Beynd financial coaching application ("the App") and website at withbeynd.com ("the Site").

1. Who we are

Beynd Ltd is a company registered in England and Wales. Our registered address is on file at Companies House. You can contact us at hello@withbeynd.com.

2. What data we collect

Beynd is designed to keep your data on your device. The App stores all financial information — income, expenses, debts, goals, investments, and payments — locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data does not leave your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

When you use the Site or App we may collect:

  • Device and usage data — browser type, operating system, pages visited, time spent. Collected anonymously for analytics purposes.
  • Contact information — if you contact us by email, we collect the information you provide in that communication.
  • Notification preferences — if you enable reminders, we store your preference locally on your device.

We do not collect your financial data. We do not have access to what you enter into the App. Your income, debts, goals, and spending figures are stored only on your device.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide and improve the App and Site
  • To respond to enquiries you send us
  • To understand how the App is used so we can make it better
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data to third parties. Beynd products are ad-free.

4. Legal basis for processing

Under UK GDPR, our legal bases for processing are:

  • Legitimate interests — to operate and improve our service
  • Contract — to provide the service you have requested
  • Consent — where you have explicitly provided it, such as for notifications
  • Legal obligation — where we are required to process data by law

5. Data storage and security

Your financial data is stored locally on your device. It is not transmitted to or stored on our servers. You can delete all data at any time from within the App using the Reset All Data option in Settings.

Any data we do hold — such as email communications — is stored securely and accessed only by authorised personnel.

6. Third party services

The App currently operates without third party integrations for financial data. When bank connectivity is introduced in a future version, we will update this policy with full details of data sharing arrangements and obtain your explicit consent before connecting any account.

The Site uses Google Fonts for typography. Google may collect limited data as a result. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com.

7. Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@withbeynd.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8. Cookies

The Site uses minimal cookies — only those necessary for the site to function. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. No cookie consent banner is required under the current configuration.

9. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us at hello@withbeynd.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App develops — particularly when bank connectivity is introduced. We will notify users of material changes by updating the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, through an in-app notice.
