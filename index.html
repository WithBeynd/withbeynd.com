On the surface, finances look fine — money in, money out. Underneath, debt costs more than you think, goals drift, and subscriptions run for things you forgot. Beynd breaks that open. You see what is happening in specific numbers and a clear plan you can follow.

What Beynd does

Built to guide what you do next

Beynd does not move your money. It coaches, analyses, and guides — before every moment that matters.

Subscription visibility
Seven days before a subscription renews, you see it — with the annual cost and a clear decision. Cancel before it charges or keep it if you use it. Your money, your call, at the right time.
Plan before the moment
A monthly plan from your income, debts, goals, and spending — adapted to who you are. Irregular earner, family, debt focus: the plan shifts because your life is not generic.
Coaching that adapts
When you follow a suggestion, the coach moves forward. When you do not, it adjusts instead of repeating. It learns from how you live — not how you meant to.
Overdue alerts
Payments and goals past their date get named with a clear next step — not a generic warning, a specific nudge when it still helps.
Debt intelligence
You see what your highest-interest debt costs each month and year, plus the overpayment that could cut years off it. Real numbers, real impact.
Your data. Your device.
Everything you enter stays on your phone: no server, no cloud account, no data sold. A coach, not a data business. Bank integration is coming — until then, your financial life stays private.

1. Introduction

Beynd Ltd (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the Beynd application.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Beynd.

Beynd is designed with a privacy-first, local-first architecture, meaning your financial data remains under your control.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Financial Data (Local Only)

When you use Beynd, you may input financial information such as:

  • income
  • expenses
  • debts
  • savings goals
  • investments

This data is:

✔ stored locally on your device
✔ not transmitted to Beynd servers
✔ not accessible by Beynd Ltd

We do not collect, store, or process this financial data on our systems.

2.2 Technical and Usage Data

We may collect limited non-financial data, such as:

  • anonymised usage analytics
  • device type and browser information
  • performance and error diagnostics

This data is used solely to:

  • improve application performance
  • identify bugs
  • enhance user experience

3. Open Banking and Third-Party Data Access

Beynd may offer optional bank connection features through regulated Open Banking providers such as TrueLayer, Plaid, or Yapily.

3.1 How Connections Work

  • Bank authentication is handled entirely by the third-party provider
  • Beynd does not see or store your banking credentials
  • Connections are established only with your explicit consent

3.2 Data Received

If you connect a bank account, Beynd may receive:

  • account balances
  • transaction history
  • account identifiers

This access is:

✔ read-only
✔ permission-based
✔ user-controlled

3.3 How Data Is Used

Connected data is used solely to:

  • provide financial insights
  • improve budgeting and planning
  • power coaching features

We do not:

  • sell this data
  • share it with advertisers
  • use it for profiling

3.4 Data Storage and Processing

Depending on implementation:

  • Data may be processed locally on your device, or
  • Temporarily processed via secure integrations with providers

In all cases:

  • Beynd does not store raw banking credentials
  • Data access is limited to what you authorise

3.5 Revoking Access

You can revoke access at any time:

  • through the third-party provider
  • or within the Beynd application

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable under UK GDPR, we process data based on:

  • Consent — for Open Banking connections
  • Legitimate interests — for improving the application

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal or financial data.

We only share data where necessary:

  • with Open Banking providers (e.g. TrueLayer, Plaid, Yapily)
  • with infrastructure providers (e.g. hosting, analytics)

All third parties are required to handle data securely and in accordance with applicable regulations.

6. Data Retention

  • Locally stored data remains on your device until you delete it
  • Uninstalling the application removes locally stored data
  • We do not retain centralised financial records

Where limited technical data is collected, it is retained only as long as necessary for operational purposes.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect data.

However:

  • no system can be guaranteed completely secure
  • you are responsible for keeping your device secure

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • access your personal data
  • request correction or deletion
  • withdraw consent
  • lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Because Beynd stores financial data locally, you retain direct control over that data.

9. Children’s Privacy

Beynd is not intended for users under 18.

We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Updates will be reflected by the “Effective date” above.
